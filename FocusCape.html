Focus Cape

The Focus Cape was a project designed to clearly project whether a wearer is busy or open to communication - in order to prevent unwanted interruptions

Using an Adafruit Circuit Playground MCU, we programmed LED functionality for visual signalling, combo'd with audio feedback with a speaker. These features were controlled by a digital on/off button, as well as an analog potentiometer for volume
