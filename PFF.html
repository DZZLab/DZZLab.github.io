The Fashion Library

The Fashion Library is design made to prevent clothing waste and increase the longevity of clothing items.
Our team site is linked HERE.

As part of a team of 3 Mechanical engineers and 2 Electrical/Computer engineers, I created the entirety of the Graphical User Interface (GUI) and its Mechatronic control of the central display rack's stepper motor.

A link to my GitHub repository: https://github.com/DZZLab/CGIvsAI_detection

This project was incredibly time consuming; and immeasurably rewarding when we got it working - receiving 2nd place in the AOM section of the Pitt 2023 Design Expo.
