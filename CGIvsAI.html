CGI vs AI Classifier

The CGI vs AI Classifier is my program made for the purpose of detecting whether an input video is made with CGI or Generative AI. Utilizing Pytorch and Pytorch Lightning libraries, I implemented a 3D Convolutional Neural Network for training and classification.

A link to my GitHub repository: HERE

My model checkpoint was trained on 50 AI videos from mostly SoraAI and 50 random CGI clips off of YouTube. I got to 75% classification accuracy on input testing datasets of 10 AI videos and 10 CGI videos.
